Move Any Mountain is an incubator for artists and artistic projects funded direclty from the JMES blockchain, with a focus on electronic music producers, labels and events.

Our mission is to dismantle traditional gatekeepers in the music industry, enabling artists to earn a living and secure funding to kickstart their careers through the decentralized funding features of the JMES blockchain.

Any kind of art that aligns with our mission can be incentivized if our community votes for it.

Whether you want to propose ideas, make music, manage & promote events, or improve or work for the Mountain DAO itself, check the bounties below or join the community in our Discord server.
